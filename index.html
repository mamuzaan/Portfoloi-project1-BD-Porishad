About Us!

Year 1980, It was a small city Uppsala in Sweden. Bangladesh Porishad Club start with 20 members. Then day by day join more and more Bengali People in this club and now about 500 members there.

  • Bangladesh Porishad Cultural Club 42 years old now. Every year, BD Porishad do diffenent kind of cultural program ex. Barbeque Party, Picnic to different place, Fishing, sports, drewing for kids, Bengali cultural program, and more.
  • Every week meet together allt clubs members and discuss about how it will be better work.
  • All the BD Porishad accept there new members. Every adult people have yearly fee 50/- sek.
  • BD Porishad get extra economic help from government.
  • Bangladesh Porishad's goal to spread its own cultural knowledge in Swedish society. To grow understanding between Bengalis and Swedes. To cooperate with local authorities of interest to the association's members as well as all people from all over the world.
